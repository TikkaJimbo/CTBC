Ranking Metrics

OBS: Overall Batting Score (Depends on BCR, BDF, CPI, SE, SRE, PPI, DOI)

BCR: Boundary Conversion Rate (Depends on boundary balls and runs in boundaries)

BDF: Batting Dominance Factor (Depends on Average, Strike Rate, Dot ball %)

CPI: Clutch Performance Index (Depends on performance as Last Man Standing)

SE: Shot Efficiency (Middling>Edging>Missing. When a shot is not offered it is not considered here)

SRE: Strike Rotation Efficiency (Depends on runs scored while running)

PPI: Powerplay Impact (Depends on Avg and SR in the Powerplay)

DOI: Death Over Impact (Depends on Avg and SR in the Death overs)
