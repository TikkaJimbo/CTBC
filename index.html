Welcome to the Ranking Tables, where cricket stars shine bright based on their all-round performance!

Bowling: Bowling strike rate, economy rate, and dot ball percentage are key metrics.

Fielding: Run outs earn more points than catches.

The MVP points are based on overall performances in batting, bowling, and fielding.
